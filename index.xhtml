The Battle for Wesnoth is a free and open-source fantasy-themed turn-based strategy game, available for Linux, Microsoft Windows, Apple OS X, and other operating systems, featuring both single-player campaigns and online or hotseat multi-player battles.

Build up a great army, gradually turning raw recruits into hardened veterans to form a formidable host against which none can stand. Choose units from a large pool of specialists, and hand-pick a force with the right strengths to fight well on diverse terrain against all manner of opposition. Engage in a desperate struggle to reclaim the throne of Wesnoth from a usurper. Step into the boots of a young officer sent to guard a not-so-sleepy frontier outpost. Save your village from the undead warriors unleashed by a foul necromancer, and rescue your brother from the sorcerer’s dungeon. Wage a brutal campaign to unite the orcish tribes. Turn to the reviled art of necromancy to defend your home from the orcs. Lead your people from the ruins of their meteor-wracked village in an epic quest to find a new home. And if that’s not enough, you can easily download a wealth of community-made content from our add-ons servers.

Want to learn more about the game? Read our Getting Started page and the Battle for Wesnoth manual.

Our community is quite friendly and helpful, so if you want to get help with something in particular, or if you want to just chat, feel free to join our forums, or drop in to our IRC channel. See also our Support page, and our (somewhat out-of-date) FAQ page.

If you encounter a bug, please help us fix it, by reporting it to us.

Various publications have written reviews of Battle for Wesnoth, but they’re mostly long out-of-date.

The Battle For Wesnoth 1.11.6 released

Saturday, 2013-08-24

The Battle For Wesnoth 1.11.6 has been released. This is the sixth release of the 1.11.x development series. Please read this forum thread for a rough idea of the changes. We also provide two changelogs — a rather nice to read players’ changelog that only includes changes most players will notice, and the (mostly) complete changelog with (almost) all the details, which may cause a serious headache....

At the moment only the Windows package is ready. You can find it on the download page. Once the others are done you will be able to find them on the download page as well. Please note that this is a development release, which may include many bugs. If you find a bug, please help us fix it, by reporting it.

The Battle for Wesnoth 1.10.7 released

Saturday, 2013-08-24

The Battle for Wesnoth 1.10.6 has been released. This is a bugfix release for the stable 1.10.x series. For information about other changes in this release, please visit this forum thread. We also provide two changelogs — a rather nice to read players’ changelog that only includes changes most players will notice (and often feels empty because developers tend to forget to add their changes there) and the (mostly) complete changelog with (almost) all the details, which may make your head spin due to the technical terms used every now and then....
